General Pediatrics

Our dedicated team of experienced Peditricians at Child central clinic are at your service.We provide comprehensive healthcare to children from newborns to the adolescent age group.Our services include newborn care,growth and developmental assessments, vaccinations,diagnosis and treatment of all Pediatric illnesses such as fevers,cold,flu,stomach pain,diarrhoea and respiratory tract infections.

Pediatric Dentistry

Good dental hygiene in children is an essential prerequisite for good dental health in adulthood.Our team of specialised pedo dontists deliver both preventive measures and comprehensive treatments for all dental problems in children.We do dental fillings,fluoride treatments,root canals and extractions etc.

Pediatric Nutrition

You are what you Eat. At Child Central clinic we assess your child for nutritional status and any nutritional risks.Based on the parameters we devise and give you dietary plans that will help resolve nutritional issues in your child.We also guide children with food allergies.We help with newborn feeding,lactational problems and pediatric eating disorders.

Pediatric Surgery

Our Pediatric Surgery department consults and guides on various pediatric surgical conditions such as congenital anomalies,pediatric urology,pediatric neurosurgery, pediatric,neonatal surgery,pediatric GI surgery etc

pediatric endocrinology

The growth,maturation and various vital functions of a child’s body is controlled and regulated by hormones.We at child central clinic diagnose and treat various various hormonal disorders of childhood such as Pediatric diabetes,pubertad disorders,obesity related disorders and growth related disorders

⁠Pediatric Dermatology

Our dermatologist at Child central Clinic diagnose and treat skin problems diseases in children of all age groups.Our doctors use their expertise in systematic evaluation of your child’s skin problem and guide you on the management of the skin condition.

Speech therapy and audiology

Pediatric speech and language therapy is to enhance children's communication skills, in both verbal and nonverbal aspects in children with language and speech issues .These issues can often coincide with mental or behavioral conditions, such as anxiety or attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD),autism spectrum disorder, and such children may encounter difficulties in expressing themselves verbally. At Child central clinic our therapist we specialize in providing personalized treatment plans ensuring that children can effectively communicate and engage with the world around them.

⁠Allergy asthma testing

⁠Eye testing

Eye testing is essential for detecting vision problems and eye conditions early. Regular exams help assess eye health, prescribe corrective lenses, and monitor for issues like glaucoma, cataracts, or macular degeneration.

⁠Pediatric health check up

Pediatric health check-ups are vital for monitoring children's growth and development. Regular visits ensure vaccinations are up to date, assess health issues, and provide guidance on nutrition and healthy habits
